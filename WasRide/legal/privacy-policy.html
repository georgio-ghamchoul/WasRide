Privacy Policy

Application: Wasl Ride  ·  Operator: Georgio Ghamchoul  ·  Last updated: 3 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Georgio Ghamchoul (“we”, “us”, the “Operator”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Wasl Ride mobile application (the “App”), a service that connects riders with drivers for local transportation and delivery. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — your phone number (used for sign-in via one-time SMS code), your name, and an optional profile photo.
  • Driver information — if you register as a driver, additional details you provide such as vehicle type and any documents required for approval.
  • Location data — your device location is collected to show nearby drivers, set pickup and drop-off points, and track a ride in progress. With your permission, drivers’ location may be collected in the background during an active trip so riders can follow the ride. We do not track your location when you are not using the service.
  • Ride and usage data — pickup/drop-off locations, ride status, prices offered and agreed, ratings, and chat messages exchanged between rider and driver for a trip.
  • Device and technical data — basic device information and a push-notification token so we can send you ride and account notifications. If a crash or error occurs, diagnostic data may be collected to help us fix problems.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and verify your identity by phone.
  • To connect riders and drivers, calculate routes and estimated times, and complete trips.
  • To send you notifications about your rides, messages, and account status.
  • To enable in-app communication (chat and, where available, calls) between rider and driver.
  • To maintain safety, prevent abuse, enforce our Terms, and respond to support requests.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the reliability of the App.

3. Payments

Wasl Ride does not process electronic payments. All fares are arranged and paid in cash directly between the rider and the driver. We do not collect or store any credit card or bank account information.

4. How Your Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only as needed to operate the service:

  • Between riders and drivers — when a ride is arranged, the rider and driver see each other’s name, photo, relevant location, and can communicate for that trip.
  • Service providers that operate parts of the App on our behalf, including: Supabase (database, authentication, and storage), Expo (app delivery and push notifications), Sentry (crash and error monitoring), and map/routing providers (Apple Maps on iOS, Google Maps on Android, and OpenStreetMap-based routing) used to display maps and calculate routes.
  • Legal reasons — if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of users or the Operator.

5. Location Permissions

The App requests location access to function. You may grant “while using the app” or background access. Background location is used for drivers during active trips so riders can track the ride. You can change or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings, though some features will not work without it.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account and ride information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us at the email below. Some information may be retained where required for legal, safety, or dispute-resolution purposes.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including access controls on our database. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

8. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw consent for optional data uses. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
